About Us

Pulong Santol is a vibrant and growing barangay dedicated to fostering a strong sense of community, progress, and development. Located in the heart of Porac, our barangay is home to hardworking and resilient residents who contribute to the area’s rich culture and traditions. With a commitment to sustainable growth, public service, and community welfare, Pulong Santol continuously strives to improve infrastructure, education, and livelihood opportunities for its people. Guided by unity and cooperation, we aim to build a safe, inclusive, and prosperous environment for all.
